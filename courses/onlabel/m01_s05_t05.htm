Gating refers to whether the learner is required to perform some action before advancing to the next screen.

A course may be gated on content and/or audio.

If a course is gated on content, the learner must complete all Knowledge Checks and must open all popups and subscreens.

If a course is gated on audio, the learner is required to listen to all audio.

If a course is gated on both content and audio, the learner must complete all Knowledge Checks, must open all popups and subscreens, and must listen to all audio.

This is a brief overview of gating. For more information, speak with your Project Manager.
