There are several different screen templates from which to choose. The templates you select will be based upon many different criteria. This demo is designed to enable you to visualize how your content and designs will fit into the course framework.

Things to think about as you move through this section:

  • Does the text (if any) fit on the screen comfortably, allowing the user to readily absorb all the information presented in one "chunk", or might it be better to spread the topic across 2 or more pages?
  • Does the graphic (if any) fit on the screen in such a way that the learner can easily see all the elements?
  • Are there any interactions that might enable the learner to engage with the material more readily and therefore retain the information more robustly?

Those questions are the starting point in determining how to proceed when developing content. Among many other factors, you MUST be keenly aware of screen real estate in order to provide the optimal learning envoronment.
