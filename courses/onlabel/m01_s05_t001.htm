Let's take a step back for a moment and talk about gating.

If a learner is required to listen to the complete audio script before moving on to the next screen, we say the course is gated on audio.

If a learner must view all parts of a page before moving on to the next screen, we say the course is gated on content. This means viewing all screens in every sequence build, selecting all popup and subscreen links*, and completing all knowledge checks.

* There are some exceptions, which we'll discuss later in this demo.
