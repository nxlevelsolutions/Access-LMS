There are only a handful of template "types", with variations based on the size of the graphic:

  • Text_only: text takes up the entire screen
  • Graphic_only: a graphic takes up the entire screen
  • Graphic_right_xx: a graphic takes up the right xx% of the screen; the remainder is allocated to text
  • Sequence_editor_text_only: one or more sequential blocks of text take up the entire screen
  • Sequence_editor_graphic_only: one or more sequential graphics take up the entire screen
  • Sequence_editor_xx: one or more graphics take up the right xx% of the screen; the remainder is allocated to text. The graphic(s) and/or text appear in sequence in time with the audio. 
  • Hotspots_xx: similar to a graphic_right_xx but with the inclusion of hotspots (we'll cover hotspots later in this demo)

The next few pages will demonstrate how screen real estate is allocated in each of the template types.
