As explained earlier, the template name indicates the percentage of the screen dedicated to the graphic.

The previous page was a Graphic Right 50: half the screen was a graphic. You may have noticed that the graphic size on a graphic right 50 screen is 984px x 1030px.

This page is a Graphic Right 60: 60% of the screen is a graphic. Since the overall width of an unresized course window is 1024px, and the framework takes up 20px on the left and right, 60% of the remaining space is 590px. Graphics are always doubled in height and width. Therefore, the width of a graphic right 60 is 1180px.

Not shown is a Graphic Right 40: 40% of the screen is a graphic. The width of a graphic right 40 is 787px.
